About Swasthya

Swasthya is an AI-driven public health chatbot designed to spread disease awareness and provide accurate health information to communities. Our goal is to make health knowledge accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere.

Our Mission

We believe that access to accurate health information should be a fundamental right, not a privilege. Our mission is to bridge the gap between medical expertise and communities through innovative AI technology.

By providing reliable, easy-to-understand health information in multiple languages, we empower individuals to make informed decisions about their health and well-being.

Our platform is designed to be accessible even in low-connectivity areas, ensuring that everyone can benefit from our services regardless of their location or technical capabilities.

Our Values

We strive to make health information available to everyone, regardless of location, language, or technical ability.

All our information is verified by medical professionals and updated regularly to reflect the latest health guidelines.

We believe in the power of community health and work to create tools that benefit entire populations.
